### Series Description
Is it possible to build real love in a world of digital appearances and hidden wounds? 📱💔

In the era of hyperconnectivity, paradoxically, we find ourselves more disconnected than ever from the soul of others. We seek perfection in edited screens and forget that true love requires vulnerability, hard work, and solid foundations.

**Hearts Without Filters** is a series of practical and spiritual guides designed for those who wish to stop surviving in their relationships and start living them with purpose. Through an approach that integrates behavioral psychology with the eternal wisdom of faith, author Eduardo Fonseca proposes a journey of profound transformation. ✨

In this series, you will learn to:

* **Tear down erroneous perceptions:** Identify how digital "filters" and prejudices cloud our ability to see the essence of people. 🚫
* **Heal so you don't hurt others:** Perform a courageous introspection to transform past pain into wisdom and personal responsibility. ❤️‍🩹
* **Build upon the rock:** Establish bonds based on transparency, interdependence, and the spiritual values that make a relationship truly lasting. 🪨
* **Exercise inner leadership:** Surrender pride and masks to love with the maturity and respect that God designed for us. 🙏

Each installment of the series is structured as a manual for progressive transformation, ideal for being read over seven-day periods, allowing each reflection to settle into concrete actions and real habit changes. 🗓️

It is time to take off the armor, heal the heart, and learn to look at others through the eyes of grace. Start today to build the relationship you not only desire for your future but that also honors your present. 🌟
