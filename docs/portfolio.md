## 🚀 Featured Projects (Software & Simulation)

### **Project HTH (Help The Homeless)**
*Social awareness through immersive technology.*
* **Description:** A 3D simulation videogame designed to raise awareness among the adult population regarding the diverse causes of homelessness. The goal transcends merely showing the problem; it aims to empower society members to become part of the solution through a deep understanding of the simulation.
* **Impact:** Teaches active ways to help that go beyond financial contributions.
* **Link:** [View Project](https://shr.pn//PHb9)

### **FairPlay**
*Accelerating innovation and development.*
* **Technologies:** .NET, Blazor, Microservices Architecture.
* **Description:** A platform designed to accelerate app development. It includes real-world templates (video sharing, dating apps), reducing costs and time-to-market for startups and established businesses.
* **Link:** [View Project](https://shr.pn//owbR)

### **Customer Finder**
*Cloud-based Business Intelligence.*
* **Technologies:** Microsoft Azure, PowerBI, Social Media Integration.
* **Description:** A solution focused on identifying potential customers by leveraging the power of social network profiles. It utilizes Azure for data processing and PowerBI for generating strategic, per-customer reports.
* **Link:** [View Project](https://shr.pn//4PvH)

### **Mood Detector**
*Technology at the service of mental health.*
* **Description:** A system focused on helping parents and psychologists identify potential signs of suicidal behavior in children by analyzing social network data for early, compassionate intervention.
* **Links:** [Demo 1](https://shr.pn//vxli) | [Demo 2](https://shr.pn//pMyv) | [Demo 3](https://shr.pn//E5PA)
